*Representing my home country at the International Physics Olympiad (IPhO) in Indonesia.*

In 2017, I traveled to Indonesia to represent Sri Lanka in the Physics Olympiad. It was warm and full of amazing culture, especially in Bali and Yogyakarta. This trip was my first time meeting other "science nerds" from all over the world, and it sparked my love for international research.
